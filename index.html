Good to know

Common questions.

What is Faith Driven Singapore?

We're the local Singapore chapter of the global Faith Driven Entrepreneur movement — a growing community of Christ-following founders and business builders who gather to worship, learn, and grow together. It's free to be part of.

Who is it for — can I join?

It's for Christ-following entrepreneurs, founders, and builders in Singapore — whether you're just starting out or scaling up. Everyone is welcome, and you don't need to come with a group.

How much does it cost?

Nothing. No cost, no catch. Our gatherings and Foundations Groups are free — the community is sustained by volunteers and monthly partners.

What happens at a gathering?

Worship, a founder's story, and honest conversation with people who understand the journey. We meet monthly across Singapore, both online and in person.

What is a Foundations Group?

An 8-week journey with 10–15 entrepreneurs through the Marks of a Faith Driven Entrepreneur, using the video series featuring J.D. Greear and Henry Kaestner. You can join online or in person.

How do I get started?

Message us on WhatsApp, join our WhatsApp community group, register with Faith Driven Entrepreneur, or simply come to the next gathering — every link you need is on this page.
